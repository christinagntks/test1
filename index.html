What are the different types of taco shells?

The two types of tacos are softshell tacos and hardshell tacos. There is a great debate over which type is better, but I prefer softshell because they are easier to eat and don't crack when you bite into them. Taco shells can be made with wheat flour or without wheat flour - this is great news for anyone with a gluten allery, intolerance, or celiac disease. Gluten-free taco shells are primarily made with corn flour
